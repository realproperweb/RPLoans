Fixed Rate

  • Rate stays same for fixed period (1-5 years)
  • Predictable repayments
  • Protection against rate hikes
  • Usually higher than variable initially
  • Early termination penalties may apply

Variable Rate

  • Rate fluctuates with prime rate
  • Potential savings if rates drop
  • More flexibility (can switch or pay extra)
  • Repayments may increase
  • Usually lower initial rate

We consider several factors:

  • Your gross monthly income
  • Existing debt obligations
  • Living expenses
  • Credit history

As a general rule, your total monthly debt repayments (including the new bond) shouldn't exceed 35-40% of your gross monthly income.

Use our online calculator or schedule a consultation for a personalized assessment.
